Fried Rice


A recipe for frozen fried rice, reheated and seasoned.

  • Frozen fried rice
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce
  • Japanese bbq sauce
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Place pan over medium high heat
  2. Oil Pan
  3. Add half a bag of fried rice to pan
  4. Cook for 4-5 minutes
  5. Move all fried rice to one side of the pan
  6. Crack 2 eggs and scramble
  7. Salt eggs and add to pan
  8. Mix eggs and fried rice
  9. Add soy sauce and japanese bbq sauce
  10. Mix for 30 seconds
  11. Add MSG and mix for another 2 minutes
  12. Plate and enjoy
