Pan Seared Steak


Here is a recipe for a Pan Seared and butter based steak seasoned with salt, pepper, garlic, and italian seasoning.

  • Rib eye steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Place skillet over medium high heat
  2. Salt and pepper steak
  3. Rend the fat until a nice brown
  4. Cook one side for one minute
  5. Flip and cook the other side for one minute
  6. Lower heat to medium low
  7. Place butter in skillet, let melt
  8. Place garlic and italian seasoning in skillet
  9. Base for one minute each side
  10. Take out of skillet and let rest for 5 minutes
  11. Enjoy
